Domain: Festivals as experience
Title: Festivals Durga Puja

Durga Puja in Bengal is a sensory explosion. It is the rhythmic, hypnotic beat of the dhak (drums) vibrating in your chest. It is the scent of shiuli flowers and incense smoke thick in the damp October air. Massive, unimaginably intricate idols of the Goddess are created only to be dissolved back into the river. It feels like a ten-day fever dream of art, devotion, food, and the fierce, triumphant presence of the feminine divine.
