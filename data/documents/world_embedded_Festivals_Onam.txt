Domain: Festivals as experience
Title: Festivals Onam

Onam is the golden harvest of Kerala. The air smells of coconut oil, jasmine, and fresh banana leaves. Intricate carpets of fresh flowers (Pookkalam) are laid at the threshold of every home. The backwaters erupt with the furious energy of snake boat races. It culminates in the Sadhya, a massive feast of 26 dishes served on a plantain leaf, creating a profound feeling of abundance, gratitude, and communal harmony.
