Domain: Music as emotion
Title: Ragas Bhairavi

Raga Bhairavi is the quintessential morning raga, yet it is almost universally played at the very end of a concert. It uses all 12 notes. It carries the immense, sweet sadness of leave-taking. Imagine a candle burning down, the scent of jasmine lingering in the air, and the feeling of something profoundly beautiful coming to an end. It evokes the feeling of devotion tinged with separation.
