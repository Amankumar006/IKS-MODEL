Domain: Music as emotion
Title: Ragas Yaman

Raga Yaman is the raga of the early evening, just as the lamps are being lit. It evokes Shringara—a deep, romantic yearning. It feels like standing by a window at dusk, waiting for someone you love, feeling both the vastness of the twilight and the intimate ache in your chest. It unfolds slowly, majestically, wrapping the listener in warmth.
